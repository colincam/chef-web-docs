To add a data bag:

#. Open the Chef management console.
#. Click **Policy**.
#. Click **Data Bags**.
#. Click **Create**.
#. In the **Create a Data Bag** dialog box, enter the name of the data bag.

   .. image:: ../../images/step_manage_webui_policy_data_bag_add.png

#. Click **Create Data Bag**.

.. end_tag
